运营后台访问vpn — 隐私说明

生效日期：2026-07-16

本扩展是公司内部工具，仅供公司授权员工在受管设备上使用，不面向公众。

它做什么

  • 将单一公司后台域名的流量经公司代理访问；其余网站直连，不受影响。
  • 为发往该公司后台的请求附加一个内部员工标识头，仅用于公司自身的访问审计。

数据处理

  • 员工标识与代理账号由公司管理员通过企业策略下发，仅本地存储于设备，只用于向公司代理认证、以及为访问公司后台的请求打标以便审计。
  • 本扩展不收集、不出售、不向开发者或任何第三方传输个人数据。
  • 本扩展不追踪其他网站的浏览行为，只对上述单一公司后台域名生效。

联系方式

如有疑问，请通过公司内部 IT 渠道联系。

Privacy Policy — Operations Backend Access

Effective date: 2026-07-16

This extension is an internal company tool for authorized employees on managed devices only. It is not intended for the general public.

What it does

  • Routes traffic for a single company backend domain through the company proxy; all other websites go direct and are unaffected.
  • Adds an internal employee-identifier header to requests sent to that company backend, used solely for the company's own access auditing.

Data handling

  • The employee identifier and proxy account are provisioned by the company administrator via enterprise policy and stored locally on the device only. They are used only to authenticate to the company proxy and to tag requests to the company backend for auditing.
  • The extension does not collect, sell, or transmit personal data to the developer or any third party.
  • The extension does not track browsing on other websites; it operates only on the single company backend domain above.

Contact

Questions: please contact your company IT department internally.
